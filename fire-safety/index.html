Fire Safety

Tonight, we tried something a little different. We setup a couple of firepits in the carpark to teach the Cubs about fire safety.

One of our Senior Sixers took small groups of Cubs through fire lighting skills and how to do things safely. They then built small tepee or log cabin fires and light them.

While this was going on we had another fire pit where we toasted some marshmallow, made smors and made campfire popcorn! This worked out so much better than we thought! So much fun tonight.
