Dorset and Somerset Air Ambulance Visits

GSL 18/10/2024 Cubs
Last night at Cubs we had the privilege to welcome volunteers from Dorset and Somerset Air Ambulance to visit us. We had a great time learning about CPR and what to do in an emergency first aid situation. It was such an informative night all round, culminating with making some Paper Helicopters.
 
 
Special thanks to Alfie who went out at the weekend to raise over £30 in donations that we gave to Dorset and Somerset Air Ambulance. If you would like to donate, please follow this link https://www.dsairambulance.org.uk/.../donate.../credit-card
