Chief Scout Silver award

GSL 24/09/2024 Cubs
What an honour tonight. I was able to award the Chief Scout's Silver Award to two of our Cubs tonight for the first time. Cameron and Toby have worked really hard in the last few months to finish off all the challenge badges with the aim for Silver to be awarded at Cub camp which happened last weekend.
Its great to watch them grown and learn new skills through Scouting and to award them their Chief Scout's Silver Award is amazing.
Well done.
