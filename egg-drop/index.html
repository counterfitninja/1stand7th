Egg Drop

We took part in an egg drop Eggperiment. The Cubs were given raw eggs that would be dropped from different heights. Each time the "passenger" had to survive the drop. If they survived, they would go even higher. The Cubs did really well, and we have 2 survivors dropping from over 3 m in height.
